About Us

Elishba Pest Control Private Limited(EPCPL) is a leading Pest Control company with over 20 years of expertise in the Pest Control Industry. We deliver our services to major Hotel Brands and Restaurants, in and around Indore, as well as to numerous Government and Private commercial sectors. Founded by Mr. Thomson Kuriakose, the company has evolved under his leadership into a industry leader and has become A Trusted Name In The Industry.

Our Objective

At EPC we aim to deliver utmost customer satisfaction, contentment and peace of mind. In our pursuit of customer happiness, we proudly leverage our well-experienced staff and modern insecticides, which positions EPC as an industry leader.
